About OTTO Lab

OTTO Lab (Observational Target Trial Operations) uses causal inference to address long-standing debates and real-world problems. Rigorous causal workflows clarify what works, for whom, when, and in which context - questions that matter for science, policy, and understanding cultural diversity.

We are based in the School of Psychological Sciences at Te Herenga Waka - Victoria University of Wellington, and is affiliated with the Centre for Applied Cross-Cultural Research (CACR).

We also maintain Places Map, an open research infrastructure for mapping places of worship and supporting cross-cultural policy research. The underlying codebase is available at github.com/go-bayes/places-of-worship.

Approach

We combine causal inference workflows with domain expertise to:

  1. Identify and state clearly defined causal questions
  2. Design studies that answer these questions
  3. Clearly communicate findings
